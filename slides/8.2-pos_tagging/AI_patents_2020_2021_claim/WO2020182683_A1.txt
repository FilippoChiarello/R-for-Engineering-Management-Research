<title>
DEVICE FOR DETECTING TEMPERATURE PROFILES, AND RELATIVE MONITORING SYSTEM
</title>

<abstract>
A device (1) aimed to the detection of temperature profiles comprises: at least a hermally isolated and watertight case (2), housing at least a processor board (CPU), wireless multihop communication subsystems, global position detection subsystems, at least one antenna, at least one external probe including one or more temperature sensors; at least one battery and related charging subsystems including a PV panel, a device buoyancy system devised to keep it in predefined floating position; and a low power sensor array capable to wake up the CPU board from a minimum power consumption state when a floating condition is detected, so that the device transmits all the data still to be transmitted and position data.
</abstract>

<claims>
1. Device (1) for the monitoring of temperature profiles including:
• at least a thermally isolated and watertight case
(2), including at least a CPU board, a wireless multihop communication systems and a global positioning detection system with at least an antenna which, when not active, are put in a minimum power consumption state;
• at least a stand (3) for external probes or sensors including one or more temperature sensors;
• at least a battery and a related charging system including a photovoltaic panel;
• a floating system, allowing to keep said case (2) in a preset position in case floating;
• a low power sensor array connected to said CPU board, including at least an internal temperature sensor, a water presence sensor and a ground contact sensor, emitting signal to awake said wireless multihop communication systems and said global positioning detection system from said minimum power consumption state, wherein said CPU board include a data sampling, processing, storage, routing and transmission software, which is capable to detect and manage a data stream related with anomaly conditions and related reactive actions, so that, when said internal temperature and ground contact sensor detect water contact and a floating condition, the global positioning detection sensor is activated, sending the positioning data and data still to be transmitted as well.
2. Device (1) according to claim 1, comprising a flashing light (8) which, un a floating condition, is positioned at the top of the device. 3. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein, inside the case (2) a thermal accumulation device is placed, to provide an additional thermal capacity so to mitigate internal thermal excursions.
4. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein at least a battery providing power supply is built up by a chemical electrical power accumulation system, preferably based on Lithium-Polymer type.
5. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein internal sensory units include at least a low power temperature sensor, a water presence sensor, a relative humidity sensor, a hygrometer and a ground contact sensor.
6. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein the said sensors are embedded in a plastic casing characterized having a high resistance to mechanical and Environmental stress .
7. Device (1) according to claim 6, wherein said plastic casing houses additional sensors selected among temperature sensors, preferably thermal resistance sensors (RTD) , humidity sensors, optical sensors for detecting the presence of water or snow, extensometers .
8. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein couples of primary sensors are provided, each sensor of the couple being provided for the detection and the monitoring of a primary parameter comprising temperature and deformation, and secondary sensors or anomaly detection units including hygrometers and optical sensors for detecting the presence of water or snow.
9. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein said wireless communication systems includes a satellite communication systems.
10. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein said software includes and implements computational intelligence algorithms devoted to local detection of monitored conditions anomalies and/or to locally forecasting relevant conditions.
11. Device (1) according to claim 10, wherein said computational intelligence algorithm is based on shallow or deep neural networks .
12. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein said software manages a photovoltaic energy surplus exploiting thermal accumulation capabilities. 13. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein said software includes the sampling frequency as a preset input parameter, to gradually increase, as a function of the variation of the relevant monitored parameter, and decrease whenever the charge state of the said battery decrease under preset alert threshold levels.
14. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein a sampling management phase include the sampling through sequential polling of the N sensors included in the external probe.
15. Device (1) according to claim 11, wherein the data storage and transmission phase use a solid state memory device (SSD) and, when in sufficient transmission and power conditions, it transmit the data through a multihop communication network to the base receiving station, while when in anomalous conditions data still to be transmitted are cached and transmitted as soon as optimal conditions are restored.
16. Device (1) according to claim 1, wherein the low power consumption sensor array is connected to the CPU board through IRQ lines. 17. Monitoring system (10), comprising several multiple device (1) for temperature profiles monitoring of any of the previous claims.
