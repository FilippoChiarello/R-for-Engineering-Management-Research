<title>
DEVICE FOR RECOGNIZING CHARACTERS AND METHOD FOR RECOGNIZING CHARACTERS BY MEANS OF SAME
</title>

<abstract>
Disclosed is a method for recognizing characters in input data by means of a device for recognizing characters. A method for recognizing characters, according to one embodiment, comprises the steps of: inputting input data into a character detection model; obtaining location information of a word area in the input data on the basis of output data outputted by the character detection model; extracting, from the input data, partial data corresponding to the obtained location information; and inputting the partial data into a character recognition model and recognizing characters in the partial data.
</abstract>
