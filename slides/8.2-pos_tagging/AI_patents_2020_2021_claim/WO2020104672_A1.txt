<title>
SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR SIMULATING A ROTATIONAL MOULDING PROCESS
</title>

<abstract>
Computer simulation system configured for determining at least one simulation variable on the basis of a predetermined rotational moulding thermal characteristic of a raw material to be used for the rotational moulding of an object and a simulation of the rotational moulding of the object by means of the virtual rotational moulding system. Computer-implemented method for using the computer simulation system.
</abstract>

<claims>
1. A computer simulation system (2) for simulating a rotational moulding process, comprising a virtual rotational moulding system comprising:
a virtual mould (3) having a mould wall surrounding a mould cavity,
a virtual mould heating and cooling system (4) at least partly integrated with the mould wall and provided for applying a temperature-time program to the virtual mould, and
a virtual motion system (5) provided for applying a motion-time program to the virtual mould; characterized in that the computer simulation system is configured for determining at least one simulation variable (8) on the basis of a predetermined rotational moulding thermal characteristic of at least one raw material to be used for the rotational moulding of an object and a simulation of rotationally moulding the object from said at least one raw material by means of the virtual rotational moulding system. 2. The system according to claim 1 , wherein the at least one simulation variable (8) comprises at least one suitable temperature-time program (42).
3. The system according to claim 2, wherein the virtual mould (3) and the integrated part of the virtual heating and cooling system (4), integrated with the mould wall, are defined on the basis of a selected one of said at least one suitable temperature-time program (42).
4. The system according to claim 2 or 3, wherein the computer simulation system is provided for determining a suitable number and location of heating and/or cooling elements to be integrated in or on the virtual mould.
5. The system according to claim 2 or 3, provided for repeating the simulation using the defined virtual mould (3) and integrated part of the virtual heating and cooling system (4).
6. The system according to any one of the claims 1 to 5, wherein the at least one simulation variable comprises at least one suitable motion-time program (43).
7. The system according to any one of the claims 1 to 6, wherein the simulation system (2) comprises a raw materials database (41 ) containing data sheets of a plurality of raw materials, each of said data sheets comprising for each raw material at least one rotational moulding thermal characteristic.
8. The system according to claim 7, wherein each rotational moulding thermal characteristic is segmented and each segment is approximated by a mathematical function. 9. The system according to claim 8, wherein at least one segment is approximated by a second order, third order, or higher order mathematical function.
10. The system according to claim 8 or 9, wherein each segment and/or transitions between the segments are determined based on at least one raw material thermal property.
11. The system according to any one of the claims 7-10, wherein each data sheet contains flow characteristics of the respective raw material and wherein the simulation system uses the flow characteristics in determining the suitable motion-time program.
12. The system according to any one of the claims 1-11 , wherein the simulation system comprises a user interface (9), configured for enabling a user to define an object to be moulded by means of a rotational moulding process. 13. The system according to any one of the claims 1-12, wherein a list of choices of temperature-time programs and/or motion-time programs, and/or combinations thereof, is generated by the simulation system.
14. A method for operating a rotational moulding simulation system according to any one of claims 1-13, comprising the steps of:
a. selecting a raw material;
b. the simulation system retrieving a predetermined rotational moulding thermal characteristic of said raw material from a database;
c. the simulation system determining at least one suitable temperature-time program and at least one suitable motion-time program for the rotational moulding of an object by means of a rotational moulding system on the basis of at least the predetermined rotational moulding thermal characteristic of the selected raw material.
15. A computer-implemented method for simulating a rotational moulding process using a computer simulation system according to any one of claims 1-13, comprising the steps of determining at least one simulation variable on the basis of a predetermined rotational moulding thermal characteristic of a raw material to be used for the rotational moulding of an object and a simulation of the rotational moulding of the object by means of the virtual rotational moulding system .
</claims>
