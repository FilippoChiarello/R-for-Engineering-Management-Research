<title>
METHOD OF PROVIDING DISCOUNTED ITEMS TO AN INFLUENCER
</title>

<abstract>
In a method for providing discounts to influences, a discount engine uses the credential information of an influencer to verify the identity of the influencer and access information relating to the followers of at least one of the influencer' s social media accounts. The discount engine provides the influencer with a code to share with the followers to track purchases of one or more inventory items. Based on the purchases made by the followers using the code, the discount engine provides the influencer with a discount on an inventory items. The discount engine triggers provision of the inventory item to the influencer.
</abstract>

<claims>
What is claimed is:
1. A method of providing an inventory item to an influencer, based at least in part upon evaluation of effectiveness of the influencer in modifying purchasing behaviors of followers of the influencer, the method comprising:
using credential information to verify identity of the influencer;
using social media data of the influencer to identify at least a first one of the
followers;
providing the influencer with a code to share with the first one of the followers;
providing the influencer with a discount on the inventory item, wherein the discount is based at least in part on tracking of purchases made by the first one of the followers when using the code; and
triggering provision of the inventory item to the influencer.
2. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is a number of subscribers to the influencer.
3. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is a number of submissions on a social media platform by an influencer.
4. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is at least one of a subscription rate and an unsubscription rate.
5. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is at least one or an average amount of time spent by a subscriber on a submission to a social media platform by the influencer.
6. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is sales data associated with the influencer.
7. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is an aesthetic theme associated with the influencer.
8. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is at least one of a categorical affiliation of the influencer.
9. The method of claim 1, wherein the social media data is an organizational affiliation of the influencer.
10. The method of claim 1, wherein the inventory item is branded with a mark of the influencer.
11. The method of claim 1, wherein the inventory item is not branded with a mark of the influencer.
12. The method of claim 1, wherein the discount is determined by a seller when the inventory item is sold.
13. The method of claim 1, wherein triggering provision of the inventory item to the influencer further comprises physical delivery of the inventory item to the influencer in a store.
14. The method of claim 1, wherein the discount is determined automatically when the inventory item is sold.
15. The method of claim 1, wherein the discount is limited by number of discounts provided to the influencer over a period of time.
16. The method of claim 1, wherein the discount is limited by total value of discounts provided to the influencer over a period of time.
17. The method of claim 1, wherein the discount is determined in part by a category of goods to which the inventory item belongs.
18. The method of claim 1, further comprising providing the influencer with monetary compensation based at least in part on the tracking of the purchases made by the followers when using the code.
19. The method of claim 9, wherein the compensation is at least one of access to privileged services and access to exclusive products.
</claims>
