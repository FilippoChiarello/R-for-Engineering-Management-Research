<title>
AUTONOMOUS LAWN MOWER BLADE SHARPENING AND TASK ROBOT
</title>

<abstract>
A lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot that autonomously drives under a lawn mower including a body, at least one motor, and a microprocessor. The robot also includes at least one a camera for object recognition and visualization of at least one of the lawn mower, a lawn mower deck, a lawn mower blade, and a lawn mower blade cutting edge, and at least one a sensor to sense the lawn mower, the lawn mower deck, the lawn mower blade, and lawn mower cutting edge. The robot may also include at least one of a task tool, a task arm, a task tip, and a task element in order to complete tasks such as sharping lawn mower blades and cleaning lawn mower blades.
</abstract>

<claims>
1. An autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot configured to drive under at least one of a lawn mower and a lawn mower deck, the robot comprising: a. at least one camera; b. at least one sensor; c. at least one task arm; and d. at least one task tip.
2. The autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot of claim 1, further comprising at least one means for object and spatial recognition.
3. The autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot of claim 1, wherein the at least one task tip is configured to sharpen at least one blade of the lawn mower.
4. The autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot of claim 1, wherein the at least one task tip is configured to clean at least one blade of the lawn mower.
5. The autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot of claim 1, wherein the at least one task tip is configured to condition at least one blade of the lawn mower.
6. The autonomous lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot of claim 1, further comprising a body, at least one motor, and at least one microprocessor.
7. A method of servicing at least one lawn mower blade of at least one of a lawn mower and a lawn mower deck, the method comprising: providing at least a lawn mower deck having at least one lawn mower blade coupled thereto; providing a lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot, the robot comprising: a. at least one camera; b. at least one sensor; c. at least one task arm; and d. at least one task tip; positioning the lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot below the lawn mower deck; positioning the at least one task tip relative to the at least one lawn mower blade so as to service the at least one lawn mower blade; and servicing the at least one lawn mower blade by way of the at least one task tip.
8. The method of claim 7, wherein servicing the at least one lawn mower blade comprises sharpening at least one blade edge of the at least one lawn mower blade.
9. The method of claim 7, wherein servicing the at least one lawn mower blade comprises cleaning the at least one lawn mower blade.
10. The method of claim 7, wherein servicing the at least one lawn mower blade comprises conditioning the at least one lawn mower blade.
11. The method of claim 7, further comprising removing the lawn mower blade sharpening and task robot from below the lawn mower deck when upon completion of the servicing of the at least one lawn mower blade.
</claims>
