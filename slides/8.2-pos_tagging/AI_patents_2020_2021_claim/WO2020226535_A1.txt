<title>
METHOD AND SYSTEM OF GENERATING INDIVIDUAL DIETARY RECOMMENDATIONS BASED ON ANALYSIS OF MICROBIOTA COMPOSITION
</title>

<abstract>
The invention relates to the field of microbiology. Proposed is a method and a computer system for generating a dietary intervention recommendation for a user. The method includes receiving, on a server, a set consisting of a suggested dietary intervention for a user and data on his/her intestinal microbiota; extracting, from a database of dietary interventions, data sets on a change to the composition of intestinal microbiota in subjects following a dietary intervention; sorting each of said subjects into a group with a high response level (responder) or a low response level (non-responder) to the received dietary intervention; generating, on the basis of the subject data and the response group, an algorithm for predicting the level of individual response to a dietary intervention; and classifying the user as a responder or non-responder and generating a dietary intervention recommendation for the user.
</abstract>
