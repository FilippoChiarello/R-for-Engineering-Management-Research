<title>
VEHICLE CONVERSATION INFORMATION OUTPUT DEVICE AND CONVERSATION INFORMATION OUTPUT METHOD
</title>

<abstract>
A vehicle conversation information output device is included in a vehicle conversation information output system configured to output conversation information to a driver driving a vehicle, the conversation information being created according to a predefined personality, the device including: a receiving part that obtains usage information related to how the vehicle has been used by the driver; a modifying part that modifies the predefined personality based on the usage information obtained by the receiving part; a creating part that creates conversation information to be output to the driver based on the personality modified by the modifying part; and an outputting part that outputs the conversation information created by the creating part to the driver.
</abstract>

<claims>
1. A vehicle conversation information output device configured to output conversation information to a driver driving a vehicle, the conversation information being created according to a predefined personality, the device comprising: an obtaining part that obtains usage information related to how the vehicle has been used by the driver; a modifying part that modifies the predefined personality based on the usage information obtained by the obtaining part; a creating part that creates conversation information to be output to the driver based on the personality modified by the modifying part; and an outputting part that outputs the conversation information created by the creating part to the driver.
2. The vehicle conversation information output device according to claim 1, wherein the usage information includes experience-based variable information that varies with increasing experience of the driver in using the vehicle.
3. The vehicle conversation information output device according to claim 2, wherein the experience-based variable information includes at least one of a cumulative travel distance, a cumulative travel time, cumulative years of use, a cumulative record of traveled areas, and a cumulative time of travel at a predetermined range of speeds.
4. The vehicle conversation information output device according to claim 1, wherein the usage information includes driving tendency information representing the tendency of driving operations performed by the driver during travel.
5. The vehicle conversation information output device according to claim 1, wherein the modifying part is configured to stop modifying the personality when a predetermined condition is satisfied.
6. The vehicle conversation information output device according to claim 1, wherein the usage information includes vehicle body information related to a vehicle body of the vehicle.
7. A vehicle conversation information output method for outputting conversation information to a driver driving a vehicle, the method comprising: obtaining usage information related to how the vehicle has been used by the driver; and outputting conversation information to the driver based on the obtained usage information, wherein the usage information includes experience-based variable information that varies with increasing experience of the driver in using the vehicle.
8. The vehicle conversation information output method according to claim 7, further comprising: storing experience information in memory, the experience information being related to an experience undergone by the driver during travel; and outputting the stored experience information.
</claims>
