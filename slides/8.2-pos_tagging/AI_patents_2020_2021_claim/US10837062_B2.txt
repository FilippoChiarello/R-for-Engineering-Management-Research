<title>
Method for predicting a treatment response to a CRHR1 antagonist and/or a V1B antagonist in a patient with depressive and/or anxiety symptoms
</title>

<abstract>
A method of treating a human patient with depressive and/or anxiety symptoms which includes administering an effective amount of a V1B receptor antagonist and/or CRHR1 antagonist to the patient in need thereof, wherein the patient's genome has certain polymorphoric variants.
</abstract>
